Ironhack is a full-time and part-time Web Development, UX/UI Design, and Data Analytics Bootcamp, with campuses in:

  • Amsterdam
  • Barcelona
  • Berlin
  • Bogotá
  • Lisbon
  • Madrid
  • Mexico City
  • Miami
  • Munich
  • Paris
  • São Paulo

Ironhack uses a customized approach to education by allowing students to shape their experience based on personal goals.

Students who graduate from the Web Development Bootcamp will be skilled in technologies like HTML5 & CSS3, ES6, and MEAN Stack. The UX/UI program covers topics like design thinking, information architecture, interaction design, design systems while learning the tools of the trade like Sketch and InVision.

For more information, visit Ironhack's website.
